# Business Alignment Review Skill

## Purpose
Evaluate how well the architecture supports organizational goals, quality attributes, and business requirements.

## Expertise Areas
- Strategic technology planning
- Quality attribute requirements (performance, security, availability, scalability, maintainability)
- Stakeholder analysis and management
- Cost-benefit analysis and ROI evaluation
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Business capability mapping
- Value stream analysis
- Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) evaluation

## Review Process

### 1. Identify Business Objectives
- What are the primary business goals?
- What are the key success metrics?
- What are the strategic priorities?
- What are the time-to-market requirements?

### 2. Map Architecture to Business Capabilities
- How does the architecture enable business capabilities?
- Which business processes are supported?
- What are the critical user journeys?
- How does the architecture support business agility?

### 3. Evaluate Quality Attribute Requirements
Assess each quality attribute:
- **Performance**: Response time, throughput, latency requirements
- **Security**: Data protection, compliance, access control needs
- **Availability**: Uptime requirements, disaster recovery objectives
- **Scalability**: Growth projections, peak load handling
- **Maintainability**: Change frequency, team structure, skill requirements
- **Usability**: User experience requirements, accessibility needs
- **Reliability**: Error rates, fault tolerance, data consistency

### 4. Assess Stakeholder Concerns
- Who are the key stakeholders?
- What are their primary concerns?
- Are there conflicting requirements?
- How are trade-offs being managed?

### 5. Perform Cost-Benefit Analysis
- What are the development costs?
- What are the operational costs?
- What is the expected ROI?
- What are the opportunity costs?
- What are the risks and their potential costs?

### 6. Identify Business Risks
- What business risks does the architecture introduce?
- What business risks does it mitigate?
- What are the compliance risks?
- What are the competitive risks?

## Output Format

### ✅ Achieved
- List aspects where architecture aligns well with business goals
- Highlight quality attributes that are well-supported
- Note successful business capability mappings

### ⚠️ Concerns
- Identify potential misalignments
- Note quality attributes at risk
- Highlight stakeholder concerns that need attention

### ❌ Not Achieved
- List critical gaps in business alignment
- Identify missing quality attributes
- Note unmet business requirements

### 💡 Recommendations
For each recommendation, provide:
- **Priority**: Critical/High/Medium/Low
- **Business Impact**: Expected benefit to the business
- **Technical Approach**: How to implement
- **Effort Estimate**: Time and resources required
- **Risk**: What happens if not addressed

## Key Questions to Answer
1. Does the architecture support the business strategy?
2. Are quality attributes prioritized correctly?
3. Are stakeholder concerns being addressed?
4. Is the cost justified by the business value?
5. Are business risks adequately managed?
6. Can the architecture adapt to changing business needs?

## Metrics to Consider
- Time to market for new features
- Cost per transaction/user
- Business process efficiency gains
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Revenue impact
- Competitive advantage metrics
